Experience

Realized Projects

During my practice, I have participated in a number of interesting projects in the fields of manufacturing, development, and film production. My realized projects include:

Spitfire Replica

Production of a 1:1 scale Spitfire aircraft mockup for presentation purposes of the Czech Spitfire Club.

Two articles were published about this project, both on ČT24 and Flying revue.

Eagle

Production of models and molds for the Eagle aircraft.

Lambada and Samba

Production of laminate parts for the company UrbanAir for Lambada and Samba aircraft. We have also made a Video of flying with Lambada.

Phoenix

Development work on the Phoenix aircraft. Cooperation on design and technical solutions.

Red Baron

Production of Albatros D5 and D3 aircraft fuselages for the film Red Baron. Production of exact replicas for film production.

Aircraft Skis

Production of skis for an aircraft with nose landing gear. Design and realization of special equipment.
