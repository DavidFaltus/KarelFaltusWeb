How we handle your personal data (GDPR).

1. Basic Provisions

The controller of personal data according to Article 4, point 7 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data (hereinafter: "GDPR") is Karel Faltus (Aero Composites).

2. Purposes of Processing Personal Data

We process your personal data for the purpose of:

  • Handling your inquiry or question from the contact form.
  • Fulfillment of the contractual relationship between you and the controller.
  • Marketing purposes (if you have given your consent).

3. Types of Processed Data

We process the following data: name and surname, email address, phone number, and possibly billing details.

4. Your Rights

Under the conditions set by the GDPR, you have the right to access your personal data, the right to rectification, the right to erasure, and the right to object to processing.
