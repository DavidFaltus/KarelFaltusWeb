About Us

Welcome to the world of Aero Composites. We are a family company with a deep passion for aviation and precise craftsmanship. Under the leadership of Karel Faltus, we specialize in the production of faithful mockups, propellers and aviation accessories that combine history with modern technologies. Each of our products is an original, into which we put our many years of experience and sense of detail.
